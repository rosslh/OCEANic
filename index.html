What is OCEANic?

OCEANic is, in essence, a personality quiz that attempts to assign percentages for ten different personality traits. You will be presented by a statement, and then you will answer with your opinion on the statement, from Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree, with each answer slightly affecting your scores. At the end of the quiz, your answers will be compared to the maximum possible for each value, thus giving you a percentage. Answer honestly!
